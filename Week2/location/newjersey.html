About Piscataway

Brief Information

New Jersey is a diverse and vibrant state located in the northeastern region of the United States. Within this state lies the township of Piscataway, which is a part of Middlesex County. Piscataway is known for its rich history, being one of the oldest townships in New Jersey, dating back to the late 17th century.

Piscataway is strategically situated, offering easy access to New York City and Philadelphia, making it a desirable location for both residents and businesses.
